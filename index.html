London Boroughs Dashboard

The aim of this London Boroughs Dashboard is to be an easy to use source of London data from a varied array of topics. Anyone who is interested should be able to look through this window and gain a detailed breakdown of each borough.

All charts are clickable and provide varied levels of interactivity. Be sure to hit the reset button at the bottom of the page to refresh any filters that have been applied.

The data used in the Crimes per Borough section was taken from a 2016 Met Police dataset. The rest of the charts are based on a 2015 dataset found at The London Data Store.

Happy Browsing!
